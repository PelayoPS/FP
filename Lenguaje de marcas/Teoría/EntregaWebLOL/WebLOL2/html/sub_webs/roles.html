How to int in each role?

TOPLANE

This lane is one of the most difficult ones to int on because of the fact that you are alone and you and you dont have as much impact as other roles. The best way to int in this lane is to pick a champion that can die easily and come back to lane to die again like Singed or Sion. You can also pick a champion that can int in teamfights baiting the team like hard engage champs.
